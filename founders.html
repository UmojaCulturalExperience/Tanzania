Keyodo, Founder

Hello, I'm Keydo, a passionate Tanzanian cultural enthusiast and founder of Umoja Cultural Organization. Our volunteering program connects global travelers with authentic Tanzanian experiences, fostering cross-cultural understanding and community development. Join us to immerse yourself in local traditions, support meaningful projects, and create lasting memories in the heart of Africa.

My name is Lordiga, dedicated and experienced volunteer coordinator, committed to empowering local communities and enriching the volunteer experience in Tanzania. With a background in managing successful volunteer programs, he ensures that international volunteers make a meaningful difference during their time here. His passion for community development and cultural exchange drives Umoja's mission to create lasting connections between volunteers and Tanzanian communities.
